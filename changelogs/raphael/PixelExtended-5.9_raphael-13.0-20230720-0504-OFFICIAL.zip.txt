Clean Flash
- Back up all your data
- Flash Recommended Recovery
- Wipe Cache - Dalvik - System - Data - Vendor
- Flash latest firmware required by ROM
- Flash Rom
- reboot system

Dirty Flash

- Back up all your data
- Wipe Cache - Dalvik
- Flash Rom
- reboot system
